## Results

- Quarter acquisition OKR beaten by **170%** without any product marketing
- **30% average WAU/MAU** achieved in 4 weeks
- Prototype and early testing results helped Safewill secure funding

## The Problem

People lack preparation for end-of-life matters. Executors face overwhelming responsibilities while grieving, including managing digital assets, documents, and estate matters simultaneously.

![The executor problem](/images/projects/the-vault/executor-light.png)

## The Solution

A centralized platform enabling users to organize critical life information for smooth executor handover.

- **Feature 1**: Users add important matters for their executor in one location
- **Feature 2**: A guided process showing which documents are essential

![Solution overview](/images/projects/the-vault/solution-1.svg)

![Guided process](/images/projects/the-vault/solution-2.svg)

## Discovery

### Competitive Audit

The competitive audit focused on understanding how competitors structure information, what guidance they provide to users, how they handle user-to-executor handover, and their pricing structures.

![Competitive audit](/images/projects/the-vault/competitive-audit.png)

## Design Exploration

Multiple design explorations tested different layouts and user journey approaches. An initial mistake was skipping sketches and lo-fi's too quickly in favor of high-fidelity work.

![Design exploration 1](/images/projects/the-vault/design-exploration-1.svg)

![Design exploration 2](/images/projects/the-vault/design-exploration-2.svg)

![Design exploration 3](/images/projects/the-vault/design-exploration-3.svg)

![Design exploration 4](/images/projects/the-vault/design-exploration-4.svg)

A constraint was having a single frontend engineer, which created a bottleneck. The design team adapted by handing off components early while continuing design iterations.

## Key Learnings

1. **Share research findings with stakeholders** to explain design rationale.
2. **Complete lower-fidelity work** before high-fidelity refinement.

## Next Steps

Focus on improving product guidance for end-of-life and estate planning preparation, making document requirements more intuitive.
