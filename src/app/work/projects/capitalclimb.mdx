## Overview

Enable users to have a more efficient and pleasant investing experience. CapitalClimb is a solo side-hustle project with end-to-end ownership of research, ideation, flows, wireframes, mockups, hi-fi design, testing, coding, architecture, branding, and marketing.

## Results

- **20% DAU/MAU** achieved within 30 days
- End-to-end ownership of design, coding, database schema, and marketing

## The Problem

I consistently used multiple disconnected apps for my stock analysis workflow, creating friction and inefficiency in the investment research process.

![Multiple apps used for stock analysis](/images/projects/capitalclimb/apps-light.svg)

## The Solution

A consolidator application offering:

- **Screen stocks** using financial metrics filtering
- **Visualize financials** for companies worldwide
- **Add notes and valuations** without leaving the platform

## Key Learnings
